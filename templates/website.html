DIGIBRAILLE




Welcome to DigiBraille! Our web app allows you to input a note (i.e. grocery list, phone number, to-do list), a recipe you
would like to make, product you would like to use, or a link to an article and directly print it to physical braille from your
phone! To start click the notes button and start writing whatever you want or the search box to look up a product or submit a link.
